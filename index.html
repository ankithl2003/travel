Bengaluru

Bengaluru (also called Bangalore) is the capital of India's southern Karnataka state. Known as the Silicon Valley of India, it's a major tech hub and also known for its parks and nightlife.

Delhi

Mumbai

Mumbai (formerly Bombay) is the big city dream of India, home to Bollywood and the largest tropical forest in an urban zone. It's famous for its vibrant street life and the iconic Gateway of India.

Goa

Goa is known for its stretches of endless silver sand and white beaches, its crystal clear waters, green mountainous highlands, and its idyllic lifestyle which embraces any newcomer into its fold.
